BANICA

  • Preparation time: 40m
  • Cooking time: 45m
  • Servings: 2 pies
Ingredients Quantity
Olive oil 1/4 cup
Eggs 3
Sour milk 2 cups
Sirene 12 ounces
Flour 1/3 cup
Banica sheets 1 pound
Unsalted butter 8 ounces

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees Grease 2 (9 inch) pie pans or springform pans with 1 tablespoon olive oil each, then dust lightly with flour.

Step 2: In a medium bowl, beat eggs. Whisk in yogurt until smooth. Crumble in feta. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt if using.

Step 3: In a small bowl, stir 1/4 cup olive oil with melted butter.

Step 4: Lightly dust work surface with flour. Unroll the phyllo sheets with short end facing you. Gently brush the top sheet with the butter mixture. Add 2-3 tablespoons of the feta yogurt in a thin line across the short side nearest you. Tightly roll up the phyllo sheet to the other short side. Place seam side down with one end in the center and spiral around in a circle. Repeat with remaining phyllo sheets, continuing to wrap them around the center until you reach the edge of the pie pan. Fill the other pie pan.

Step 5: Drizzle the top of the pies with remaining butter mixture and lightly with seltzer water to barely moisten.

Step 6: Bake in preheated oven until the top is golden brown, 45 minutes to 1 hour. Allow to cool 10-20 minutes before drizzling with honey and serving.

POTATO TORTILLA

  • Preparation time: 15m
  • Cooking time: 25m
  • Servings: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Onions 3
Potatoes 3
Eggs 7
Vegetable Oil 1 spoon
Salt 1 pinch
Pepper 1 pinch

Step 1: Peel and dice onions. Peel potatoes and cut into slices of medium width.

Step 2: Add eggs to a large bowl, whisk until creamy, and season with salt and pepper. Set aside.

Step 3: Heat a generous amount of vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add potatoes and cook for approx. 10 - 11 min. until potatoes are cooked through. Remove excess oil.

Step 4: Add onions and continue to sauté, stirring occasionally, for approx. 2 - 3 min. until softened.

Step 5: Pour in eggs to cover potatoes and cook approx. 8 - 10 min. over low heat until the bottom and the edges have set. Then run a spatula around edges of pan. Place a large plate on top. With one hand securing the plate and the other holding the handle of the frying pan, quickly flip the tortilla onto the plate and slide back into pan with raw side down. Cook on the other side for approx. 4 - 5 min. until egg is cooked through. Alternatively, transfer pan to oven and bake at 200°C/400°F until cooked through for approx. 10 min. Transfer to a plate to serve and cut into pieces.
